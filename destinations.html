Discover Amazing Travel Destinations

Siquijor is a tiny, mystical island province in the Central Visayas, famously known as the Philippines' "Island of Fire" due to its folklore, fireflies, and traditional healers (mangkukulam). As the third smallest province, it is a popular eco-tourism destination offering white sand beaches, coral-rich diving, waterfalls, and a relaxed, affordable, and, sustainable, travel experience.

Mt. Bandilaan

Mt. Bandilaan is the highest point in Siquijor and offers panoramic views of the island and surrounding sea. Visitors can enjoy nature trails, peaceful forests, and a relaxing mountain atmosphere.

Tulapos Falls

Tulapos Falls is one of the most unique waterfalls in Siquijor. Unlike most waterfalls that are wide and cascading, Tulapos Falls is tall and narrow,creating a powerful stream of water that flows between rocky cliffs. Surrounded by lush greenery, it is a peaceful spot for visitors who wantto enjoy nature and take beautiful photos.

Guiwanon Spring Park

Guiwanon Spring Park is a mangrove forest eco-park located along the coastof Siquijor. Visitors can walk along a scenic wooden boardwalk that passesthrough dense mangrove trees while enjoying fresh sea air and beautifulcoastal views. The park promotes environmental conservation and offersa relaxing nature experience for travelers.
